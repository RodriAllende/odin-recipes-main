My 3 Favorites Recipes

I WILL SHOW YOU MY 3 FAVORITES RECIPES AND HOW TO MAKE IT
Lasagna Ragu Bologna

The smooth ricotta cheese, rich tomato sauce, and soft pasta are a perfect match. I like to make my lasagna with ground meat, but it's also delicious with roasted vegetables or even a mix of both. Freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top is a must-have finishing touch to give it that extra, gratin flavor.

Burger BBQ Texas

Another of my favorite foods is the hamburger, but not just any hamburger: a juicy, spicy meat burger with a touch of curry. I love the combination of spices in the meat, along with a good amount of melted cheese, crisp onion and fresh tomato. If the burger comes with fries, even better.

Coconut Curry

One of my favorite foods is curry. I love the combination of spices and flavors in each bite. Chicken or lamb are my favorite protein options in curries, and I always ask for it to be a little spicy to add a touch of excitement to my palate. Basmati rice is the perfect accompaniment to this dish, helping to balance out the strong flavors of the curry
